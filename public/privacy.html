Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Assassins ("we", "our", "the app") is a live social elimination game. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Phone number: If you create an account, we collect your phone number solely for authentication purposes. We send a one-time verification code via SMS when you sign in. We do not use your phone number for marketing or share it with third parties.

Profile information: You may provide a display name and profile photo. These are visible to other players in your games.

Game data: We store game activity including scores, eliminations, and evidence photos you upload during gameplay.

Device identifier: We generate a random anonymous device ID stored locally on your device to maintain your game session.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to operate the game: authenticating your identity, displaying your profile to other players, tracking game stats, and enabling real-time multiplayer gameplay. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

SMS Messages

We send SMS messages only for account verification (one-time passcodes). Message frequency: one message per sign-in attempt. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out by choosing to play as a guest instead of creating an account.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS) in the US East region. Profile photos and evidence photos are stored in secure cloud storage with public read access for gameplay purposes.

Data Retention

Game data is retained for the duration of the game. Account profiles and stats are retained until you request deletion. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

• Supabase — database and authentication
• Twilio — SMS delivery for verification codes
• Vercel — web hosting

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to request data deletion, please contact us through the app.
